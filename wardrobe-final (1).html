How It Works

Getting started is simple - just follow these easy steps

1

Upload Your Wardrobe

Start by taking photos of your clothes and uploading them to StyleAI. Simply click or drag images into the upload zone. Add details for each item including the category (tops, bottoms, shoes, outerwear, accessories), color, and style (casual, formal, sporty, trendy, classic). This helps our AI understand your wardrobe better and create more accurate recommendations. Don't worry about perfect photos - just clear images that show the item clearly are enough!

2

Build Your Digital Closet

Continue adding items until you've digitized your entire wardrobe or at least your most-worn pieces. The more items you upload, the more outfit combinations our AI can create. You can organize and view your items by category using the filtering options. Your virtual wardrobe serves as a visual inventory of everything you own, making it easy to see what you have and plan outfits. You can always add new items as you acquire them or remove items you no longer own.

3

Set Your Preferences

When you're ready for outfit suggestions, navigate to the recommendations tab and tell us what you need. Select the occasion you're dressing for - whether it's a casual day out, work, a formal event, date night, party, sports, or travel. Then choose the weather conditions - sunny and warm, cool and pleasant, cold, rainy, or hot and humid. These preferences help our AI understand the context and create appropriate outfit suggestions that match both your needs and the environment.

4

Get AI-Powered Recommendations

Click the "Generate Outfit Ideas" button and watch as our AI analyzes your entire wardrobe in seconds. The AI considers multiple factors: color coordination, style compatibility, occasion appropriateness, weather suitability, and fashion principles. Within moments, you'll receive three complete outfit combinations, each showing images of the specific items from your wardrobe. Every suggestion includes a detailed explanation of why the outfit works, helping you understand the styling choices.

5

Choose & Style With Confidence

Browse through the AI-generated outfit recommendations and pick the one that resonates with you. Each outfit card displays clear images of the clothing items you need to put together, making it easy to find them in your actual closet. Get dressed knowing that your outfit is professionally styled, color-coordinated, and perfect for your occasion. The more you use StyleAI, the better you'll understand fashion principles and develop your own styling intuition!
